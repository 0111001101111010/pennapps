Instructions

  1. Download the Android app from the Google Play Store.
  2. Update your Pebble firmware to version 2.0 or higher.
  3. Download the Pebble app from Android Pebble appstore beta or manually install the pebble app.
  4. Open up Gestice from Android's homescreen.
  5. Configure your X-axis (left-right), Y-axis (up-down), and Z-axis (in-out) gestures.
  6. Lock your Android phone.
  7. Open up Gestice on your pebble.
  8. Shake your Android phone left-right, up-down, and in-out. The pebble should facing the opposite direction of the phone screen.
  9. Congrats, you've unlocked your phone!
